# Competitor Analysis — Diving Club Trincomalee

## Top 5 Competitors

### 1. International Diving School Trincomalee
- **Website**: internationaldivingschool.com
- **PADI Status**: 5-Star Resort
- **Operating since**: 1988 (37+ years)
- **Location**: Pigeon Island Beach Resort, Nilaveli
- **TripAdvisor**: #1 ranked Trincomalee diving activity since 2015
- **PADI Directory**: Listed with full profile

**Strengths**: Longest-established centre, TripAdvisor #1 badge, strong domain authority from age, PADI 5-Star credibility.

**Weaknesses**: Thin blog (no long-form content), no AI search optimization, no HMS Hermes guide, dated website design.

**What they rank for**: "scuba diving trincomalee", "diving trincomalee sri lanka", "PADI diving trincomalee"

### 2. Poseidon Diving Sri Lanka
- **Websites**: divingsrilanka.com, srilankadivecentres.com
- **PADI Status**: 5-Star IDC Center
- **Operating since**: 50+ years
- **Locations**: Nilaveli + Hikkaduwa
- **Social**: Active Facebook (@poseidondivingsrilanka), Instagram

**Strengths**: Multiple websites (domain portfolio), dual locations, social media presence, competitive pricing transparency.

### 3. Epic Ocean Adventures
- **PADI Status**: 5-Star IDC Dive Resort
